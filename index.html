Hello, I am Jiaxing Lin. I am a first-year Ph.D. student in Linguistics at the University of Texas at Arlington (UTA). This is my personal academic website for research, teaching, publications, and projects.

My research interests center on speech perception, speech learning, and listener adaptation to variability in spoken language. I am broadly interested in understanding when and under what conditions listeners adapt, how adaptation unfolds over time, what changes occur in the speech perception system, and how adaptation affects later speech and lexical processing.

I use behavioral experiments, acoustic analysis, and computational modeling to study these questions, with related interests in applications such as speech training and speech technology.
